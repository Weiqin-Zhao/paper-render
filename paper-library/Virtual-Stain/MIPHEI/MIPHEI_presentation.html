Background & Challenges

H&E staining is the most routine method in pathology but is limited to morphological information. Multiplex immunofluorescence (mIF) enables protein-level cell typing, providing critical insights into the tumor immune microenvironment, but remains expensive and not clinically widespread.

However, ViTs are designed for classification tasks, outputting fixed-resolution token sequences not directly suited for dense prediction tasks requiring pixel-level outputs. Several strategies adapt ViTs for dense prediction:

UNETR

使用转置卷积对 ViT tokens 进行上采样，生成层级特征用于 U-Net 跳跃连接。简单直接但收敛较慢。

Uses transpose convolutions to upsample ViT tokens, generating hierarchical features for U-Net skip connections. Simple but slower convergence.

ViTMatte

并行 CNN 流提取金字塔特征，ViT 仅提供瓶颈层语义信息。MIPHEI 采用此策略。

Parallel CNN stream extracts pyramidal features, ViT provides only bottleneck-level semantics. Adopted by MIPHEI.

ViT-Adapter / ViT-CoMer

在 ViT 内部实现更丰富的多尺度交互，但计算成本更高。

Enable richer multi-scale interaction within ViT, but at higher computational cost.

MIPHEI 选择 ViTMatte 策略是性能与效率的最佳折中：仅 6.6M 可训练参数，收敛速度比 UNETR 快 10 倍，同时保持甚至超越 UNETR 的最终性能。
MIPHEI's choice of ViTMatte strategy is the best trade-off between performance and efficiency: only 6.6M trainable parameters, 10x faster convergence than UNETR, while matching or exceeding UNETR's final performance.

补充说明

Additional Notes

  • 名称来源：ViTMatte 最初由 Yao et al. (2024) 提出用于图像 matting（抠图）任务。原版在 ViT 输入中拼接了一张 trimap 引导图。MIPHEI 的改进版去除了 trimap 输入、convolutional necks 和 window attention，仅保留了并行 CNN 流 + ViT bottleneck 的核心设计。
  • CNN Detail Capture 分支：独立的卷积网络从原始 H&E 图像提取多尺度金字塔特征（从高分辨率到低分辨率），作为 U-Net decoder 的 skip connection 输入。这弥补了 ViT 在所有层保持固定特征维度的缺陷。
  • ViT Token → 空间特征：ViT-G/14 将 256×256 输入切分为 14×14 = 196 个 patches（每个 patch 18×18 像素），输出 196 个 token 特征。这些 token 被 reshape 为 14×14 的空间特征图，仅作为 decoder 最底层（bottleneck）的输入。
  • 可训练参数：ViTMatte 变体仅 6.6M 可训练参数（LoRA + decoder + CNN 分支），远少于 UNETR 的 36M 可训练参数。
  • Name origin: ViTMatte was originally proposed by Yao et al. (2024) for image matting tasks. The original version concatenates a trimap guidance image to ViT input. MIPHEI's modified version removes the trimap input, convolutional necks, and window attention, retaining only the parallel CNN stream + ViT bottleneck core design.
  • CNN Detail Capture branch: An independent convolutional network extracts multi-scale pyramidal features from the raw H&E image (high-to-low resolution), serving as skip connection inputs for the U-Net decoder. This compensates for ViT's limitation of maintaining fixed feature dimensions across all layers.
  • ViT Token → Spatial features: ViT-G/14 splits the 256×256 input into 14×14 = 196 patches (each 18×18 pixels), outputting 196 token features. These are reshaped into a 14×14 spatial feature map, used only as the decoder's bottommost (bottleneck) input.
  • Trainable parameters: The ViTMatte variant has only 6.6M trainable parameters (LoRA + decoder + CNN branch), far fewer than UNETR's 36M trainable parameters.

损失函数

Loss Function

采用加权均方误差（Weighted MSE），通过可学习的通道权重 $\sigma_j$ 自动平衡各标记物通道的贡献：

Uses weighted MSE with learnable channel weights $\sigma_j$ to automatically balance contributions across marker channels:

$$\mathcal{L}_{wMSE} = \lambda \sum_j \frac{1}{\sigma_j^2} \mathcal{L}_{MSE,j}$$

其中 $\sigma_j$ 为第 $j$ 个标记物在训练集上的标准差，$\lambda$ 为重建损失的总权重。这一设计源自 inverse-variance weighting（Mai et al., 2021）——低信号标记物（如 FOXP3，前景像素少、标准差小）获得更高权重，避免被高信号标记物（如 Hoechst）主导梯度。目标值被缩放到 [-0.9, 0.9]（而非 [-1, 1]），以避免 Tanh 在极端值处的饱和效应。

Where $\sigma_j$ is the standard deviation of the $j$-th marker across the training set, and $\lambda$ is the overall reconstruction loss weight. This design originates from inverse-variance weighting (Mai et al., 2021) -- low-signal markers (e.g., FOXP3, with few foreground pixels and small std) receive higher weights, preventing high-signal markers (e.g., Hoechst) from dominating gradients. Target values are scaled to [-0.9, 0.9] (not [-1, 1]) to avoid Tanh saturation at extreme values.

不使用 GAN 判别器 -- 消融实验表明 Pix2Pix 判别器反而降低细胞级别的准确率。作者推测判别器优化"视觉真实性"而非"信号精确性"，牺牲了蛋白表达预测的准确性。
No GAN discriminator -- ablation shows Pix2Pix discriminator actually hurts cell-level accuracy. The authors hypothesize the discriminator optimizes for "visual realism" rather than "signal fidelity," sacrificing protein expression prediction accuracy.

训练细节

Training Details

  • LoRA 微调：rank=8, α=1，仅作用于注意力层的 Q 和 V 矩阵
  • 无 GAN 判别器：纯回归目标，避免模式坍塌
  • 收敛速度：ViTMatte 仅需 1 epoch 即达到 UNETR 10 epochs 的性能
  • 输出激活：多头 Tanh 激活
  • LoRA fine-tuning: rank=8, α=1, applied to Q and V matrices of attention layers only
  • No GAN discriminator: pure regression objective, avoiding mode collapse
  • Convergence speed: ViTMatte reaches UNETR 10-epoch performance in just 1 epoch
  • Output activation: Multi-head Tanh activation

自荧光校正公式

Autofluorescence Correction Formula

mIF 图像中的自荧光 (AF) 信号必须被去除以获取真正的蛋白表达信号。每个通道 $c$ 的校正公式为：

Autofluorescence (AF) signal in mIF images must be removed to obtain true protein expression signals. The correction formula for each channel $c$ is:

$$I^c_{cor} = \max(0,\; I^c_{IF} - \lambda_c \cdot I_{AF} + b_c)$$

其中 $\lambda_c$ 和 $b_c$ 为每个通道手动调整的参数，使用自定义 Napari 工具进行交互式可视化调参。$I_{AF}$ 为专门采集的自荧光通道信号。

Where $\lambda_c$ and $b_c$ are manually adjusted per channel using a custom Napari tool for interactive visualization. $I_{AF}$ is the dedicated autofluorescence channel signal.

对数归一化公式

Log Normalization Formula

校正后的荧光信号经过对数归一化，压缩高值并减少离群值影响：

Corrected fluorescence signals are log-normalized to compress high values and reduce outlier impact:

$$I^c_{norm} = 255 \cdot \log\left(\frac{\min(I^c_{cor},\; q^c_{0.999})}{q^c_{0.999}} + 1\right)$$

其中 $q^c_{0.999}$ 为训练集中每个标记物前景像素强度的 99.9 百分位值。对数变换压缩了高强度值，减少了离群值对模型训练的影响，同时保留了低信号标记物的信息。

Where $q^c_{0.999}$ is the 99.9th percentile of foreground pixel intensities per marker across the training set. The log transform compresses high intensity values, reduces outlier impact on model training, while preserving information from low-signal markers.

Pseudo-Label 生成：GMM + 层级门控

Pseudo-Label Generation: GMM + Hierarchical Gating

细胞级评估依赖于可靠的 ground truth，本文采用如下策略从 mIF 数据中提取 pseudo-labels：

Cell-level evaluation relies on reliable ground truth. The paper extracts pseudo-labels from mIF data using the following strategy:

  1. 核分割：使用在 DAPI 通道上微调的 Cellpose 模型分割细胞核
  2. 细胞近似：将核区域膨胀 2μm 作为细胞区域的近似
  3. 单细胞特征：计算每个细胞区域内各标记物通道的平均荧光强度
  4. GMM 门控：对每个标记物独立拟合高斯混合模型 (GMM)，通过后验概率区分阳性/阴性细胞
  5. 层级约束：应用生物学层级规则，例如 CD3+ 细胞必须同时为 CD45+（T 细胞是白细胞的子集），确保标记物之间的逻辑一致性
  1. Nuclei segmentation: Cellpose model fine-tuned on DAPI channel segments cell nuclei
  2. Cell approximation: Nuclear regions dilated by 2μm as cell boundary approximation
  3. Single-cell features: Mean fluorescence intensity computed per cell region for each marker channel
  4. GMM gating: Gaussian Mixture Model independently fitted for each marker, posterior probabilities distinguish positive/negative cells
  5. Hierarchical constraints: Biological hierarchy rules applied, e.g., CD3+ cells must also be CD45+ (T-cells are a subset of leukocytes), ensuring logical consistency across markers
Pseudo-label 的可靠性是一个重要的限制因素。GMM 门控对伪影、近似的细胞边界和信号溢出敏感。层级约束虽然提升了一致性，但底层 GMM 阈值本身可能存在偏差——因此模型评估的上限受 pseudo-label 质量制约。
Pseudo-label reliability is a key limiting factor. GMM gating is sensitive to artifacts, approximate cell boundaries, and signal spillover. While hierarchical constraints improve consistency, the underlying GMM thresholds may themselves be biased -- thus model evaluation is upper-bounded by pseudo-label quality.

Evaluation Methodology

The ablation study reveals four key findings:

1. GAN 判别器有害

1. GAN Discriminator is Harmful

加入 Pix2Pix 判别器后，PSNR 从 27.86 降至 27.00，Cell AUC 从 0.868 降至 0.817。作者推测 判别器倾向于生成视觉真实但不精确的图像——对"好看"的追求牺牲了对细胞蛋白表达的准确预测。

Adding a Pix2Pix discriminator drops PSNR from 27.86 to 27.00, Cell AUC from 0.868 to 0.817. The authors hypothesize that the discriminator favors visual realism over fidelity -- the pursuit of "realistic-looking" images sacrifices accurate prediction of cellular protein expression.

2. Foundation Model 选择关键

2. Foundation Model Choice Matters

H-optimus-0 (ViT-G/14, DINOv2+iBOT, 500k+ WSIs) > UNIv2 (ViT-H/14, DINOv2, 200M tiles) > CTransPath (Swin, MoCov3, 15.6M tiles)。预训练数据规模和 SSL 方法是决定性因素，CTransPath 的 Cell F1 仅 0.351，比 H-optimus-0 低 23%。

3. LoRA 优于冻结编码器

3. LoRA Outperforms Frozen Encoder

LoRA (rank=8, α=1) 微调 Q/V 投影矩阵，以仅 36M 可训练参数（1.1B 总参数的 3%）即可将 Cell F1 从 0.413 提升至 0.431。小成本、大收益。

LoRA (rank=8, α=1) fine-tunes Q/V projection matrices with only 36M trainable parameters (3% of 1.1B total), improving Cell F1 from 0.413 to 0.431. Low cost, significant benefit.

4. ViTMatte 收敛快 10 倍

4. ViTMatte Converges 10x Faster

ViTMatte 和 UNETR 最终性能相近（ViTMatte Cell AUC 0.876 略优），但 ViTMatte 仅需 1 epoch 即达到 UNETR 10 epochs 的水平。CNN 分支提供的中间金字塔特征使 decoder 重建更高效。

讨论

Discussion

优势：Foundation Model 编码器带来强泛化 Strength: Foundation Model Encoder for Robust Generalization

使用在大规模病理数据上预训练的 ViT Foundation Model (H-optimus-0) 作为编码器，使模型在仅 41 张训练 WSI 的条件下也能在外部数据集上表现优异。LoRA 微调策略进一步提升了性能，同时保持了参数效率。

Using a ViT foundation model (H-optimus-0) pretrained on large-scale pathology data as encoder enables strong external dataset performance even with only 41 training WSIs. LoRA fine-tuning further improves performance while maintaining parameter efficiency.

优势：细胞级评估框架 Strength: Cell-Level Evaluation Framework

不同于仅报告像素级指标的工作，MIPHEI 建立了完整的细胞级评估流程：预测 mIF → 细胞分割 (Cellpose) → GMM + 层级门控分类 → Cell AUC/F1 评估。这更贴近下游临床应用需求。

Unlike works that only report pixel-level metrics, MIPHEI establishes a complete cell-level evaluation pipeline: predict mIF → cell segmentation (Cellpose) → GMM + hierarchical gating classification → Cell AUC/F1 evaluation. This is more relevant to downstream clinical applications.

优势：单模型预测 15 个标记物 Strength: 15 Markers from a Single Model

一个统一模型同时预测 15 个蛋白标记物，覆盖免疫、上皮、基质、血管和增殖等类别，是目前虚拟染色工作中标记物覆盖最全面的之一。

A single unified model simultaneously predicts 15 protein markers covering immune, epithelial, stromal, vascular, and proliferation categories -- among the most comprehensive in virtual staining literature.

局限：训练数据量有限 Limitation: Limited Training Data

仅 41 张训练 WSI，虽然 Foundation Model 弥补了部分数据不足，但对于稀有标记物（如 FOXP3, PD-L1），数据中正样本极少，导致预测性能很低。

Only 41 training WSIs. While the foundation model compensates partially, rare markers (e.g., FOXP3, PD-L1) have very few positive samples in the data, leading to poor prediction performance.

局限：细胞分类器需要域适应 Limitation: Cell Classifier Needs Domain Adaptation

基于 GMM + 层级门控的细胞分类器是在 ORION 数据上校准的，应用到新域时需要一定的 mIF 校准数据来调整阈值。

The GMM + hierarchical gating cell classifier is calibrated on ORION data. Applying to new domains requires some mIF calibration data to adjust thresholds.

局限：PD-L1 几乎不可预测 Limitation: PD-L1 Nearly Unpredictable

PD-L1 的 Cell AUC 仅 0.048，几乎等于随机。这表明 PD-L1 的表达模式无法从 H&E 形态中可靠推断，可能需要其他辅助信息或专门的模型设计。

PD-L1 Cell AUC is only 0.048, nearly random. This indicates PD-L1 expression patterns cannot be reliably inferred from H&E morphology, possibly requiring auxiliary information or specialized model designs.

关键问题：临床可行性与评估可靠性 Critical Question: Clinical Feasibility & Evaluation Reliability

ORION 上 0.438 的平均 Cell F1 距离临床诊断标准仍有较大差距。作者也坦承 MIPHEI 的定位不是直接替代 mIF 用于临床诊断，而是作为 回顾性队列挖掘和假说生成的研究工具。这个定位是务实的——对于探索性分析，区域级的细胞密度相关性（IMMUcan Pearson ~0.69）可能已经足够有意义。

The 0.438 average Cell F1 on ORION is still far from clinical diagnostic standards. The authors acknowledge that MIPHEI is positioned not as a direct replacement for mIF in clinical diagnosis, but as a research tool for retrospective cohort mining and hypothesis generation. This is pragmatic -- for exploratory analysis, region-level cell density correlations (IMMUcan Pearson ~0.69) may already be meaningful enough.

更重要的隐忧是 评估的循环依赖问题：pseudo-labels 本身由 GMM 门控生成，其准确性受自荧光、信号溢出、近似细胞边界等因素限制。模型的评估上限被 pseudo-label 质量所制约——如果 pseudo-labels 对某些标记物本身就不准确，那么低 F1 可能部分反映的是 pseudo-label 噪声，而非模型的真实预测能力。

A more fundamental concern is the circular evaluation dependency: pseudo-labels themselves are generated by GMM gating, whose accuracy is limited by autofluorescence, signal spillover, and approximate cell boundaries. Model evaluation is upper-bounded by pseudo-label quality -- if pseudo-labels are themselves inaccurate for certain markers, low F1 may partially reflect pseudo-label noise rather than the model's true predictive ability.

局限：组织类型泛化未知 Limitation: Unknown Tissue Type Generalization

MIPHEI 仅在结直肠癌 (CRC) 组织上训练和验证。CRC 的组织学特征（腺体结构、淋巴滤泡、黏膜肌层）与其他肿瘤类型（如乳腺癌、肺癌、黑色素瘤）差异显著。模型是否能迁移到其他肿瘤类型完全未知，这严重限制了其实际应用范围。

MIPHEI is trained and validated exclusively on colorectal cancer (CRC) tissue. CRC histological features (glandular structures, lymphoid follicles, muscularis mucosae) differ significantly from other tumor types (breast, lung, melanoma). Whether the model transfers to other tumor types is completely unknown, severely limiting its practical applicability.

局限：计算成本 Limitation: Computational Cost

H-optimus-0 拥有 1.1B 参数，推理一张 WSI 需要处理数千个 256×256 tiles。论文未报告推理时间和 GPU 内存需求，但 ViT-G/14 的计算开销对临床部署环境（通常无 A100 级 GPU）构成实际障碍。

H-optimus-0 has 1.1B parameters, and inference on a single WSI requires processing thousands of 256×256 tiles. The paper does not report inference time or GPU memory requirements, but ViT-G/14's computational overhead poses practical barriers for clinical deployment environments (typically without A100-class GPUs).

  • Retrospective H&E cohort mining: Virtual mIF analysis on existing H&E slide collections to explore immune microenvironment spatial features without additional experiments

  • Data screening tool: Rapidly screen cases with abnormal immune infiltration patterns in large cohorts to guide subsequent mIF experimental validation

像素级指标可能不可靠 Pixel-Level Metrics May Be Unreliable

H&E 图像通常不包含细胞质边界的精确信息，因此像素级的准确性评估具有误导性。生物学上有意义的信息在于单个细胞内的蛋白表达水平，而非像素级的完美重建。这正是细胞级指标（Cell AUC、Cell F1）比像素级指标（PSNR/SSIM）更适合评估虚拟染色质量的根本原因。

H&E images are usually not informative about cytoplasmic boundaries, so pixel-wise accuracy is misleading. The biologically meaningful information lies in protein expression levels within individual cells, not pixel-perfect reconstruction. This is precisely why cell-level metrics (Cell AUC, Cell F1) are more appropriate than pixel-level metrics (PSNR/SSIM) for evaluating virtual staining quality.

未来方向：Immunoscore 预测 Future Direction: Immunoscore Prediction

将 MIPHEI 扩展到直接从 H&E 预测临床相关评分（如 Immunoscore）是一个令人期待的未来方向。当前的细胞类型空间分布预测可以被聚合为此类临床评分——Immunoscore 基于肿瘤核心和浸润边缘的 CD3+ 和 CD8+ 细胞密度，而 MIPHEI 已经能够预测这些标记物的空间分布。

Extending MIPHEI to predict clinically relevant scores like Immunoscore directly from H&E represents a compelling future direction. The current cell-type spatial distribution predictions could potentially be aggregated into such clinical scores -- Immunoscore is based on CD3+ and CD8+ cell densities in tumor core and invasive margin, and MIPHEI can already predict the spatial distribution of these markers.

结论

Conclusion

MIPHEI 利用 ViT Foundation Model（H-optimus-0）作为 U-Net 编码器，通过 ViTMatte 集成策略从 H&E 图像预测 16 通道多重免疫荧光信号。该方法在内部数据集（ORION）和两个外部数据集（HEMIT、IMMUcan）上均超越了现有 SOTA，展现出强大的跨域泛化能力。

MIPHEI predicts 16 mIF channels from H&E using ViT foundation models (H-optimus-0) in a U-Net architecture with the ViTMatte integration strategy. The method outperforms SOTA on the internal dataset (ORION) and 2 external datasets (HEMIT, IMMUcan), demonstrating strong cross-domain generalization.

性能总结

Performance Summary

  • 上皮标记物表现优异：Pan-CK（F1=0.884）和 E-cadherin（F1=0.903）预测准确度极高，因为上皮细胞在 H&E 中具有显著的形态学特征
  • 广谱免疫标记物良好：CD45（F1=0.681）和 CD3e（F1=0.572）表现尚可，淋巴细胞在 H&E 中可识别
  • 特定免疫亚型中等：CD8a、CD4、CD68、CD163 等亚型标记物性能中等，受限于 H&E 形态信息的分辨能力
  • 基质标记物尚可：SMA（F1=0.564）表现中等
  • 困难标记物：FOXP3（F1=0.114）因稀有性表现较差；PD-L1（F1=0.048）因其功能性本质几乎不可预测
  • High accuracy for epithelial markers: Pan-CK (F1=0.884) and E-cadherin (F1=0.903) achieve excellent prediction accuracy due to distinct morphological features in H&E
  • Good for broad immune markers: CD45 (F1=0.681) and CD3e (F1=0.572) perform well, as lymphocytes are identifiable in H&E
  • Moderate for specific immune subtypes: CD8a, CD4, CD68, CD163 show moderate performance, limited by H&E morphology's resolving power
  • Reasonable for stromal markers: SMA (F1=0.564) performs moderately
  • Struggles with rare/functional markers: FOXP3 (F1=0.114) is limited by rarity; PD-L1 (F1=0.048) is nearly unpredictable due to its functional nature
