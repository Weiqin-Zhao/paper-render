动机与问题

Motivation & Problem

NTP 的核心瓶颈

Core Bottlenecks of NTP

Next-Token Prediction (NTP) 是 LLM 训练和推理的主流范式，但存在两个结构性问题：

Next-Token Prediction (NTP) is the dominant paradigm for LLM training and inference, but suffers from two structural issues:

上下文覆盖有限
Limited Context Coverage

每步仅基于前文预测单个 token，短视地聚焦于相邻上下文，缺乏长程依赖建模。

Each step predicts only one token from context, myopically focusing on adjacent context without long-range dependency modeling.

推理效率低下
Low Inference Efficiency

严格的自回归串行生成，每个 token 需要一次完整的 forward pass，无法并行。

Strict autoregressive sequential generation requires one full forward pass per token with no parallelism.

$$\mathcal{L}_{\text{NTP}} = -\sum_T \log p(x_{t+1} \mid x_{\leq t}; \theta)$$

MTP 的局限：仍困于相邻位置

MTP Limitations: Still Confined to Adjacent Positions

Multi-Token Prediction (MTP) 是 NTP 的自然扩展（DeepSeek-V3、Gloeckle et al. 2024），通过多个输出头并行预测相邻的 $n$ 个 token $[t\!+\!1, t\!+\!2, \ldots, t\!+\!n]$。但预测范围仍局限于相邻位置，没有突破局部上下文的限制。

Multi-Token Prediction (MTP) extends NTP (DeepSeek-V3, Gloeckle et al. 2024) by predicting $n$ adjacent tokens $[t\!+\!1, t\!+\!2, \ldots, t\!+\!n]$ in parallel. However, the prediction range remains confined to adjacent positions, not breaking beyond local context.

L-MTP 核心思路：不预测相邻 token，而是跳跃式预测非连续位置的 token。例如，4 个头预测 $[t\!+\!1, t\!+\!3, t\!+\!5, t\!+\!7]$ 而非 $[t\!+\!1, t\!+\!2, t\!+\!3, t\!+\!4]$，用相同数量的头覆盖 2 倍的上下文范围。 L-MTP Core Idea: Instead of predicting adjacent tokens, leap to non-contiguous positions. E.g., 4 heads predict $[t\!+\!1, t\!+\!3, t\!+\!5, t\!+\!7]$ instead of $[t\!+\!1, t\!+\!2, t\!+\!3, t\!+\!4]$, covering 2x context span with the same number of heads.

方法

Method

跳跃式预测设计

Leap Prediction Design

给定输入 $x_{\leq t}$，L-MTP 以步长 $k$ 预测跳跃位置的 token 序列：

Given input $x_{\leq t}$, L-MTP predicts tokens at leaping positions with stride $k$:

$$\text{L-MTP}: \quad x_{t+1}, \; x_{t+k+1}, \; x_{t+2k+1}, \; \ldots, \; x_{t+k(n-1)+1}$$

例如 $k=2, n=4$：预测位置 $[t\!+\!1, t\!+\!3, t\!+\!5, t\!+\!7]$，跳过中间 token，覆盖 7 个位置的范围（而非 MTP 的 4 个）。

E.g. $k=2, n=4$: predict positions $[t\!+\!1, t\!+\!3, t\!+\!5, t\!+\!7]$, skipping intermediate tokens, covering a span of 7 positions (vs. MTP's 4).

两阶段训练

Two-Stage Training

Stage 1: Head Warm-up

Stage 1: Head Warm-up

冻结 backbone，仅训练新增的预测头。使用自蒸馏数据（backbone 自己生成的输出），新头按跳跃模式分配不同的监督信号：

Freeze the backbone, train only the new prediction heads. Use self-distillation data (backbone's own outputs), with new heads assigned supervision at leaping positions:

$$\mathcal{L}^{(1)}_{\text{L-MTP}} = -\sum_T \log p\bigl(x_{[t+k(n-1)+1,\ldots,t+k+1]} \mid z_{\leq t};\, \{\theta_i\}_{i>1}\bigr)$$

Stage 2: Full Model Tuning

Stage 2: Full Model Tuning

LoRA 微调全模型，联合优化 backbone 和所有预测头：

LoRA fine-tuning of the full model, jointly optimizing backbone and all heads:

$$\mathcal{L}^{(2)}_{\text{L-MTP}} = -\sum_T \Bigl[\log p(x_{t+1} \mid x_{\leq t};\, \theta', \theta_1) + \beta \cdot \log p\bigl(x_{[t+k(n-1)+1,\ldots,t+k+1]} \mid x_{\leq t};\, \theta', \{\theta_i\}_{i>1}\bigr)\Bigr]$$

其中 $\beta$ 控制额外头的贡献权重。

where $\beta$ controls the contribution weight of auxiliary heads.

Looking Backward 解码策略

Looking Backward Decoding Strategy

L-MTP 每步预测不连续的 token（有间隙）。关键洞察：间隙中的 token 已被前一步预测过。

L-MTP predicts non-contiguous tokens each step (with gaps). Key insight: the gap tokens were already predicted by the previous step.

Looking Backward：当前步预测 $\{x_{t+1}, x_{t+3}, x_{t+5}, x_{t+7}\}$，前一步（条件 $x_{\leq t-1}$）已预测了 $\{x_t, x_{t+2}, x_{t+4}, x_{t+6}\}$。通过"回看"前一步的结果填补间隙，无需额外推理，只需检索已有预测。 Looking Backward: Current step predicts $\{x_{t+1}, x_{t+3}, x_{t+5}, x_{t+7}\}$, while the previous step (conditioned on $x_{\leq t-1}$) already predicted $\{x_t, x_{t+2}, x_{t+4}, x_{t+6}\}$. By "looking backward" at previous predictions, gaps are filled at zero additional compute cost.

连续序列通过回看 $k\!-\!1$ 步获得，合并后可拼出完整的 $k(n\!-\!1)\!+\!1$ 个 token 候选序列。

Continuous sequences are assembled by looking back $k\!-\!1$ steps, yielding a complete candidate sequence of $k(n\!-\!1)\!+\!1$ tokens.

Tree Attention 集成

Tree Attention Integration

L-MTP 与 speculative decoding 无缝集成，构建层次化 token tree：

L-MTP integrates seamlessly with speculative decoding via a hierarchical token tree:

  • 第 $i$ 层代表第 $i$ 个预测头的候选 token
  • 探索 tree 中的路径找到被接受的序列
  • 使用 tree attention mask 限制每个 hidden state 只关注其祖先节点
  • Layer $i$ represents candidate tokens from the $i$-th prediction head
  • Explore tree paths to find accepted sequences
  • Tree attention mask restricts each hidden state to attend only to its ancestors

理论分析

Theoretical Analysis

两个核心性质

Two Core Properties

Definition 1: Attenuation（衰减性）

Definition 1: Attenuation

预测越远的 token，边际概率越低：

Marginal probability decreases with prediction distance:

$$p(x_{t+1}\!\mid\!x_{\leq t}) > p(x_{t+2}\!\mid\!x_{\leq t}) > \cdots > p(x_{t+n}\!\mid\!x_{\leq t})$$

Assumption 2: Consistency（一致性）

Assumption 2: Consistency

期望边际概率关于预测距离 $i$ 稳定且可预测：

Expected marginal probability is stable and predictable w.r.t. distance $i$:

$$\mathbb{E}_{x_{\leq t} \sim \mathcal{D}}\bigl[p(x_{t+i}\!\mid\!x_{\leq t})\bigr] = f(i)$$

Theorem 3: 更少衰减，更多加速

Theorem 3: Less Attenuation, More Speed-up

设衰减函数 $f(i) = \exp[-\gamma \cdot (i\!-\!1)]$，则当衰减系数 $\gamma = O(1/n^2)$ 时，L-MTP 的期望接受长度渐近优于 vanilla MTP：$E[L]_l > E[L]_s$。

Let attenuation function $f(i) = \exp[-\gamma \cdot (i\!-\!1)]$. When $\gamma = O(1/n^2)$, L-MTP's expected acceptance length asymptotically exceeds vanilla MTP: $E[L]_l > E[L]_s$.

直觉：L-MTP 用更长的预测范围 $k(n\!-\!1)\!+\!1$ 补偿跳跃位置的置信度损失。当模型预测较自信（$\gamma$ 小）时，L-MTP 的加速优势越显著。 Intuition: L-MTP compensates the confidence loss at leap positions with a wider prediction range $k(n\!-\!1)\!+\!1$. When the model is more confident (small $\gamma$), L-MTP's speedup advantage grows.

This suggests L-MTP's benefits for larger models may face diminishing returns.

可深入讨论的问题

Discussion Points

k=2 的普适性？ Universality of k=2?

论文默认 $k=2$，但不同任务/模型可能有不同的最优 $k$。自适应 $k$ 的选择是关键的未来工作。是否可以基于局部 entropy 动态调整步长？

The paper defaults to $k=2$, but different tasks/models may have different optimal $k$. Adaptive $k$ selection is a key future direction. Could stride be dynamically adjusted based on local entropy?

训练数据规模是否太小？ Is training data scale too small?

Stage 2 仅用 10K 样本微调。在更大规模数据上，L-MTP 的优势是否更明显？还是说小数据量本身就是方法的优势（低成本适配）？

Stage 2 uses only 10K samples for fine-tuning. Would L-MTP's advantage grow with larger data? Or is the small data requirement itself an advantage (low-cost adaptation)?

与 DeepSeek-V3 MTP 的关系 Relationship with DeepSeek-V3 MTP

DeepSeek-V3 已大规模部署 MTP 进行预训练。L-MTP 能否直接替换其解码策略？从头用 L-MTP 目标预训练是否能带来更大收益？

DeepSeek-V3 has deployed MTP at scale for pretraining. Can L-MTP directly replace its decoding strategy? Would pretraining from scratch with L-MTP objectives yield larger gains?

推理加速的竞争力 Speedup Competitiveness

Speedup ratio 多数在 1.5–2.5×，相比 speculative decoding with draft model 的 3–4× 是否有竞争力？L-MTP 的优势在于不需要额外的 draft model，这在实际部署中可能更有价值。

Speedup ratios of 1.5–2.5× compared to 3–4× from speculative decoding with draft models. L-MTP's advantage is no need for a separate draft model, which may be more valuable in practical deployment.

Myopia 悖论对大模型的影响 Myopia Paradox Impact on Large Models

大模型在远距离预测上更差，这是否意味着 L-MTP 对 70B+ 规模模型的收益会递减？如何用 L-MTP 目标进行预训练来缓解近视性？

If larger models are worse at distant prediction, does L-MTP's benefit diminish at 70B+ scale? Could pretraining with L-MTP objectives mitigate the myopia?

局限性

Limitations

  • 实验限于 ≤12B 模型（计算资源限制）
  • $n$ 和 $k$ 的选择目前是固定的（$k\!=\!2, n\!=\!4$），未自适应
  • 数据质量对结果影响大（部分情况下 NTP 微调也会降低 base 性能）
  • Experiments limited to ≤12B models (compute constraints)
  • $n$ and $k$ are currently fixed ($k\!=\!2, n\!=\!4$), not adaptive
  • Data quality significantly affects results (NTP fine-tuning can degrade base performance in some cases)
